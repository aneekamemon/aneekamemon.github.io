About FROST
THE PROCESS
FROST takes pleasure in the thorough research and careful selection that goes into assembling every clock we offer. Our team is always watching horological developments, global market trends, and collector preferences to make sure every watch satisfies our high standards for quality, design, and value. We work directly with reliable merchants and each item is carefully examined. Every watch at FROST, whether it's a timeless classic or a limited-edition release, is chosen for its craftsmanship as well as its ability to complement our customers' individual tastes and lifestyles.
VERIFICATION & AUTHENTICATION
Every timepiece goes through a rigorous multi-step verification and authentication process conducted by our team of experts. From examining serial numbers and movement integrity to inspecting craftsmanship details like dial markings and case engravings, we ensure that each watch meets the highest standards of originality. We also cross-reference items with official brand records and consult with certified horologists when necessary.
CONDITION & HISTORY
Every watch is meticulously examined, graded, and, if required, professionally serviced to guarantee best performance and look. We obviously state the condition—whether it's like-new, gently worn, or vintage—so consumers know precisely what to expect. All eligible watches come with a FROST-issued warranty, which offers coverage and support long after purchase, therefore supporting our dedication to quality. Our aim is to provide not just great watches but also unrivaled peace of mind.
